Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you register, as well as the expense records you log — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the core features of Spendly: displaying your expenses, calculating spending summaries and category breakdowns, and keeping your account secure. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and are only accessible to you through your authenticated session. Passwords are never stored in plain text. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. Any third-party services we rely on for core functionality (such as hosting) are held to the same standard of care for your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
